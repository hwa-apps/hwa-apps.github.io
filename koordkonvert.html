About KoordKonvert

KoordKonvert – The Coordinate Converter App

Easily convert between coordinate formats and visualize them on a map with KoordKonvert. Whether you're a hiker, surveyor, soldier, police officer, forester, or geospatial enthusiast, this app simplifies working with coordinates like Decimal Degrees (DD), Decimal Degrees Minutes (DDM), Degrees Minutes Seconds (DMS), UTM (Universal Transverse Mercator), MGRS (Military Grid Reference System), and even regional systems like EOV (Hungary) – more formats coming soon!

Key Features:

  • Coordinate Conversion: Precise and OFFLINE conversions between DD, DDM, DMS, UTM, MGRS, and EOV.
  • Interactive Maps: View coordinates on OpenStreetMap, or unlock Google Maps (Normal, Satellite, Hybrid, Terrain).
  • Get Your Location: Instantly fetch your current coordinates using GPS.
  • Save & Manage: Store favorite coordinates with timestamps or custom names.
  • Copy & Share: Copy converted results to clipboard with one tap.
  • Multilingual Support: English, Hungarian, German, Spanish, Polish, Italian.
  • Customizable Settings: Input assistant, regional systems, map types, keep-screen-on mode.
  • Ad-Free Option: Remove ads and permanently unlock Google Maps.

How It Works:

  1. Enter or fetch your coordinates using GPS.
  2. Select input and output formats from dropdowns.
  3. Tap Convert to see instant results.
  4. View them on the map, copy or save for later use.
