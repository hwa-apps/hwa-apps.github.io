About apeX Timer

apeX Timer – Custom Workout & Interval Timer

apeX Timer is a powerful and highly customizable interval timer designed for workouts, fitness training, HIIT sessions, circuit training, calisthenics, CrossFit, boxing, running, stretching, and many other activities that require precise time management.

Create your own training sessions by configuring work, rest, warm-up, cool-down, rounds, sets, and custom intervals exactly the way you want. Whether you're a beginner following a simple routine or an advanced athlete managing complex workout structures, apeX Timer adapts to your training style.

Save your favorite timer configurations as presets and launch them instantly whenever you train. Name your exercises for individual sets or rounds, edit saved presets at any time, and track your workout history and statistics to stay motivated and monitor your progress over time.

With a clean interface, audio cues, vibration alerts, and extensive customization options, apeX Timer helps you stay focused on your workout instead of watching the clock.

Key Features:

  • Custom Intervals: Configure work, rest, warm-up and cool-down durations exactly the way you want.
  • Rounds & Sets: Create simple or complex workout structures with customizable rounds and sets.
  • Exercise Names: Assign custom exercise names to individual sets or rounds.
  • Unlimited Presets: Save, load and edit unlimited workout presets for quick access.
  • Workout Statistics: Track your workout history and training progress over time.
  • Audio & Vibration Alerts: Stay informed with audio, vibration and visual feedback during workouts.
  • Dark Mode: Train comfortably in any lighting conditions.
  • Simple Interface: Easy to use for beginners while offering advanced customization options.

How It Works:

  1. Set your desired work, rest, warm-up and cool-down times.
  2. Choose the number of sets and rounds, and optionally assign exercise names.
  3. Press START and focus on your workout while apeX Timer handles the timing.
  4. Save your configuration as a preset for future workouts or load an existing preset and start immediately.
