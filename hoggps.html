About Hog GPS

Hog GPS – The Hiking & Outdoor GPS App

Hog GPS is your ultimate outdoor navigation companion. Whether you are hiking, hunting, training, or exploring, Hog GPS gives you powerful tools to navigate and record your adventures. The app supports downloadable offline maps, so you can rely on it even without internet access. Track your routes, save waypoints, measure distances and areas, or even use military APP-6 symbols for professional applications. All in a lightweight and easy-to-use app.

Key Features:

  • Offline Maps: Download OpenStreetMap areas and use them anytime, even without internet access.
  • Multiple Map Types: OpenStreetMap, Google Maps (Normal, Satellite, Hybrid, Terrain, Dark Mode), plus unique game-inspired designs like GTA and Assassin’s Creed.
  • Tracking: Record your routes and view detailed paths with distance and time.
  • Waypoints & Notes: Save locations with custom markers and photos.
  • APP-6 Military Symbols: Add units, equipment, and control measures directly on the map.
  • Drawing Tools: Draw routes, circles or areas on the map, with OSM snapping support.
  • Measurements: Measure distances, perimeters, and areas directly on the map.
  • Import & Export: Work with GPX, KML, and route files (load or share tracks with others).
  • Multilingual Support: English, Hungarian, Italian, Spanish, German.
  • Customizable Settings: Choose coordinate formats: DD, DDM, DMS, UTM, MGRS, USNG (United States National Grid), OSNG (British National Grid), Gauss-Krüger, CH1903+/LV95 (Swiss Grid), and EOV (Hungarian); units; and bearing types (degrees, NATO mils, Russian mils).
